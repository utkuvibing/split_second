# Split Second v1.0 Release Notes

## EN

Split Second is here.

- Vote on daily "Would You Rather" questions against the clock.
- Play new morning, afternoon, and evening question rounds.
- Compare your answers with friends and global results.
- Unlock personality insights based on your choices.
- Earn badges, build streaks, and climb the leaderboard.
