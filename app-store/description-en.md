# Split Second

Subtitle: Daily Would You Rather

Split Second is a fun daily opinion game where every choice counts. Vote on fast "Would You Rather" questions against the clock, then see how your answers compare with friends and other players.

New questions arrive every day in morning, afternoon, and evening rounds, giving you quick moments to play, react, and discover what your choices say about you.

Unlock personality insights, earn badges for your streaks and achievements, and climb the leaderboard as you build your Split Second profile.

Features:

- Daily "Would You Rather" questions
- Timed voting for quick, instinctive choices
- Morning, afternoon, and evening question drops
- Friend comparisons and global opinion results
- Personality insights based on your answers
- Badges, streaks, and leaderboard progress

Keywords: would you rather, daily question, opinion, poll, personality, friends, game
